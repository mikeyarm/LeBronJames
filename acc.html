LeBron James
Biggest Accomplishments

LeBron James has won four NBA championships, including the 2023 NBA Cup with the Lakers. LeBron James has won the NBA Finals MVP award four times, which is considered one of the biggest individual accomplishments in basketball. LeBron James holds the record for the most assists in the NBA All-Star Game. He's also one of only two players to win the All-Star Game MVP award in their final season. LeBron James was named the Most Valuable Player in the 2023 NBA In-Season Tournament. He won three Olympic gold medals with the U.S. national team. He was named the Olympics MVP in the 2024 Summer Olympics. He was the first player in NBA history to record at least 30,000 points, 10,000 rebounds, and 10,000 assists. He opened the I PROMISE School in Akron, Ohio, in 2018, which provides free tuition, meals, and bikes to at-risk youth.
